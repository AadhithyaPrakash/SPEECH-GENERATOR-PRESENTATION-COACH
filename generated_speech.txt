**Introduction**
(0:00 - 0:30)
First, let's define what AI is. Artificial Intelligence refers to the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that would typically require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making. AI has been around for decades, but recent advancements have brought us to the cusp of a technological breakthrough.

**Body**
(0:30 - 2:30)
Now, let's talk about the recent trends in AI. One of the most significant developments is **Deep Learning**. This subfield of AI uses neural networks, inspired by the human brain, to analyze vast amounts of data. Imagine being able to recognize objects, speech, and even emotions with unprecedented accuracy!

Another trend is **Natural Language Processing**. This technology allows computers to understand and generate human-like language, making it possible for us to interact with AI systems more seamlessly. Think of AI-powered chatbots that can have conversational conversations with you!

Another exciting trend is **Edge AI**. This technology enables AI to process data on devices, rather than relying on cloud computing. This means that AI can be integrated into our daily lives, from smart home appliances to self-driving cars!
